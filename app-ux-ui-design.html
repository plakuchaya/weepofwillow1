IMPOSSIBLE ARCHITECTURE

# concept design
# 3d modelling
# ux/ui design
2021-2022

The premise of this project, named IMPOSSIBLE ARCHITECTURE, is a reflection on architecture as a form of art. Buildings surround us everywhere, even though some are used to the surrounding and pay less attention to it. Architecture creates a particular atmosphere and evokes certain emotions, just like any other form of artistic expression.

Nowadays, architecture is rarely designed for aesthetic reasons, rather it is focused on practical application. That way, it occurred to me that even though it is such a technical sphere in real life, it can be may not be in the virtual world. I wanted to explore this idea by creating an application.

Research

The main research method for this project was competitive analysis. I selected different programs that are related to 3D, drawing and architecture in order to compare and contrast their functionality, method of navigation, layout, etc. I took a look at industry standard programs such as Maya and Blender, as well as more accessible programs such as Tinkercad. Also, I examined apps such as Make a Pose, Room Planner and Procreate because of their layout and navigation methods.
