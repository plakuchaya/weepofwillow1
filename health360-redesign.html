HEALTH360

# ux/ui design
# branding
# graphic design
2021-2022

Health360 is an innovative virtual health care platform of HealthCare 365 virtual health management company. Its foundational belief is that blending a protocol-driven software platform with a high-touch personalized nursing service has been the driving force behind its success in the health care space.

The goal of this project was to redesign the website for a better user experience, improving the platform for current clients, as well as making it welcoming for new users. The project was a collaboration between HealthCare360 and George Brown College in Toronto, Canada. My team: Guilherme Sutto de Rezende, Tina Pham, Brandon Li.
