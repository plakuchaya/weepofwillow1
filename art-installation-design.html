The project named Formación (Becoming) was completed as a part of Digital Experience Design program at George Brown College in Toronto, Canada. The challenge was to reflect on a certain artwork or on a series of artworks, bringing in a new vision of the artist's oeuvre. In my case the chosen artist turned out to be Pablo Picasso and his series of self-portraits made during different life stages.
