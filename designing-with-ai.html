The project ANDERERSEITS: Die Vorurteile der Menschen gegenüber KI is inspired by the emerging advances in artificial intelligence and its ability to engage in conversations with humans, as well as producing images from defined descriptions. Regardless of its abilities, the machine still struggles with falling in love or learning from the experience. This project is an experiment of talking to AI about its its own self-perception, as well as biases people might be holding towards AI and its bodily image.
